Our Story

SmartEats was created with the vision of transforming the traditional college canteen experience. Long queues, order delays, and stock confusion inspired us to build a fast, digital, and user-friendly canteen system. Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify food ordering for students and empower canteen management with automation. We aim to reduce waiting time, improve transparency in order processing, and provide an organized, smart solution for inventory and sales tracking. SmartEats strives to create a connected ecosystem where delicious food meets seamless technology.
